MHFA Awareness Survey

How Would You Respond?

Survey Questions

1. Anxiety Attack
Your friend suddenly starts breathing fast, trembling, and says they feel something bad is happening. What would you do first?

2. Withdrawal
A friend who is usually energetic suddenly avoids everyone for the last 3–5 days. How would you respond?

3. “Nothing matters anymore”
A classmate casually says: “I don’t think anything matters these days.” What is the safest response?

4. Sudden Anger / Irritability
Someone snaps at you repeatedly for small things. What would you do?

5. Suddenly Very Happy
A person who was sad for weeks suddenly becomes overly excited and energetic. How would you see this?

6. Exam Stress Panic
Your friend freezes before an exam and says their mind is blank. What would you do?

7. Isolation in Hostel
A hostel mate has been skipping meals and staying locked in their room. How would you handle it?

8. Friend Breaking Down
A friend bursts into tears suddenly and says they “can’t handle things.” What would you do?

9. Self-harm Concern
You see risky marks on someone’s hand and they avoid the topic. What’s the safest first step?

10. Confidence Level
How confident do you feel helping someone in emotional distress?
